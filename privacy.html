Privacy Policy last updated 16 June 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how Buyza collects, uses, and safeguards your information.

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with Buyza, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, email address, phone number, shipping/billing address.
  • Payment Data: Credit/debit card details, PayPal, or other payment methods (processed securely by our partners).
  • Usage & Device Info: IP address, browser type, pages visited, referral source, and interaction data.
  • Preferences: Product preferences, wishlist items, and marketing communication choices.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the data we collect to:

  • Process orders and deliver products to you.
  • Personalize your shopping experience and recommend products you may like.
  • Improve our website, services, and customer support.
  • Send order confirmations, shipping updates, and promotional offers (only with your consent).
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or security incidents.

3. Sharing Your Information

We never sell your personal data. However, we may share it in the following cases:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors, shipping partners, and IT support that help us operate.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law, court order, or to protect the rights of Buyza and others.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (you will be notified).

4. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing, analyze traffic, and remember your preferences. You can control cookie settings in your browser, but disabling them may affect functionality.

  • Essential: Required for core site features like logging in and adding to cart.
  • Analytics: Help us understand user behavior to improve our site.
  • Marketing: Used to display relevant ads (only with your permission).

5. Your Choices & Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectify: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Delete: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations).
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time via the link in our communications.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@buyza.com.

6. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security protocols, including SSL encryption, firewall protection, and regular security audits. While no system is 100% secure, we take reasonable steps to protect your data.

7. International Transfers

If you are accessing Buyza from outside the United States, your data may be transferred to and processed in the U.S. or other countries where our servers and partners are located. By using our services, you consent to this transfer.

8. Children’s Privacy

Buyza does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover such data has been inadvertently collected, we will delete it promptly. Parents or guardians may contact us if they believe we have collected information about a child.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice on our website or via email. Continued use of Buyza after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
